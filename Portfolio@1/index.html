About Me

Arjun Poudel– Singer, Musician, Lyricist, and Producer With a diverse and dynamic career in music, I have contributed my talents as a singer, musician, lyricist, and producer across multiple projects in the Nepali music industry. As a Singer,I have released various songs across multiple platforms: -Dekhirahanchhu Jahile(2018) – Budha Subba -Mera Kala Dari (2020) – 8K Everest Motion Picture - Babaal (2019) – Arjun Poudel Channel - Hiu Jsti Gori (2020) – Arjun Poudel Channel As a Musician and Lyricist, I’ve created some memorable pieces that have resonated with many listeners: - Piyera Nasha (2019) – Arjun Poudel Channel - Manaka Kura (2021) – Arjun Poudel Channel - Yo Dil Chorera (2020) – 3D Nepali Music / Kumar Thapa Magar In addition, I’ve taken on the role of ‘Producer’ for the short movie “Chatpate Bhaiya” (2019) – Budha Subba Digital. I’ve also appeared in interviews and discussions about my work. Notable appearances include the ‘Babaal’ song recording interview on Nagarik Online TV. Explore my music journey and stay tuned for more exciting projects..
